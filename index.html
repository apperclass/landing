TASTED

TASTED is an iOS App for iPhone that helps you to find the best restaurants in your city. Users can create and share charts, follow users and navigate maps. Tasted is developed with PhoneGap, Ionic, AngularJs and the backend is powered by Symfony and AWS.

DIGITAL CLASSROOM

DIGITAL CLASSROOM is a web application powered by AngularJs, NodeJs and Symfony2 that allows teachers to create groups with their students, share files, create quizzes and manage a calendar. A custom social network made with open source web technologies with a completely responsive interface and a modern look and feel.
